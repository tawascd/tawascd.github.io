Tawa SCD Club AGM - 26 March 2026

Good afternoon

The Tawa SCD Club AGM will be held this coming Thursday, 26 March, during the club night, at around 8 pm. Of course, we would like as many members as possible to attend. Please read the following attached documents beforehand. It will save time at the meeting, so that we can quickly get back to to dancing.
  • The Agenda for the AGM
  • The Minutes of last year's AGM 
  • The Treasurer's report
  • The President's report

While most of the current committee are happy to continue, we would like to welcome a couple more people onto the committee. It is not an onerous task to be a committee member: Take part in discussions around planning for club activities, and take on small tasks to help with the running of the club. 

We have three (or sometimes four) meetings a year, held at a committee member's home. They usually take about one, or one-and-a-half hours, and are followed by a cuppa and eats. 
Last year's meetings covered the following:
In April: first meeting after the AGM - plan for the year ahead, particularly planning for the Annual General Dance in early June. Allocation of tasks. 
In July/August: Plan for club activities, e.g Beginners' classes, Dessert and Dance night, Reels class, Final night, End-of-year potluck.
In October: Plan for the following year, decide on dates for Club restart, Beginners' classes, AGM, Annual Dance, Final night.

The committee has been discussing the possibility to shift the date of the AGM from the beginning of the year (March/April) to the end of the year (October/November), in line with most other clubs. This will require a change in the way the Treasurer keeps the books (change the end date of the financial year), a change to the club's Constitution, and this will need a Special General Meeting to assess the feelings of the club membership. This could be one of the issues to be discussed this year.

Looking forward to seeing you all on Thursday
