Tawa SCD Club News - 2 March 2026

Hello everyone

This is a reminder that Club nights are about to start up again - this coming Thursday at 7:30 at the usual place. As it is the first night, we will have supper - cuppa and biscuits. 

We had a very pleasant 'welcome back' afternon tea at Caroline's on Sunday 22 February. We had 15 people all up. A big thank you to Caroline and John for hosting us again. 

Also a somewhat belated reminder of the first "walking dance" class tmorrow afternoon - Tuesday 3 March. This is aimed at people who love to dance, but find that it is a bit too physically demanding. It will be 'proper' dancing, but no bouncing skip change, or 'hops' in the strathspey. 

This class will be a try-out, to see how many are interested in pursuing this. If successful, the classes will be repeated on the first Tuesday of each month, at the St John's Anglican Church Hall, in Johnsonville (by the roundabout). The teacher will be Gaye Collin, and the door charge will be $5. I am sorry I will be missing out on this class (because of Covid - see below). 

I hope to see as many of you as possible at club on Thursday. I also hope that I will be able to attend myself, as I have just this morning come down with Covid. Hopefully by Thursday, I'll be over the worst of it. I will be wearing a mask if I do attend ...

Cheers

Desiree
