Tawa SCD Club News - 1 May 2026

Hello everyone.

We had a great night of dancing last night, and it was good to welcome Shaun, a new dancer from the Johnsonville Club.

John and  Désirée are on holiday for the next couple of weeks, enjoying the good weather while it lasts. I will be looking after the secretary role in  Désirée's absence. Please excuse any errors I may make.  

Last night’s dances
All but the last dance, Three Feisty Women, are on the programme of our upcoming Annual Dance. My favourite was The Chequered Court with all the twirling. 
  1. Dame Margaret's Jig. This was our walking warm-up dance.  There is no video available. (R&L;  cross, cast, 1/2 Fig 8; adv & ret with 1st corners, adv & ret with 2nd corners; circle.)
  2. On the Quarter Deck (R)    https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/5080/#videos    
  3. The Golden Years (S) – https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/15402/  beware - there are four dances with the same name! 
  4. Mrs MacPherson of Inveran  (R)  https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/4784/   Please note that last night, we started the dance with a standard reel on the side. The Inveran reel with the 1C crossing top and bottom will be added to the dance in the coming weeks. 
  5. Anna Holden's Strathspey (S)  https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/159/#videos
  6. The Chequered Court (J ) – This will be the final dance on our Annual Dance programme. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/10787/ 
  7. Three Feisty Women  (R)  https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/14568/#cribs
