Hello everyone

We had quite a drop in numbers tonight, compared to last week. We managed just two 5C sets, with one spare person - 21 dancers tonight. Catherine is preparing us for some of the dances on the programme of the Easter Saturday dance - more about that below. 

Tonights dances 
  1. Short and Very Sweet (J) - Walking warm-up. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/12568/ Devised by Rod Downey, for Isla and Eric Norris, longstanding members of Johnsonville Club (now both deceased), who were indeed both quite short and very sweet.  
  2. The Auld Grey Cat (R) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/295/ Devised by Iain Boyd. We did this last week. 
  3. First Love (S) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/8047/ Catherine first had us practise dancing a circle in Strathspey - emphasising that while moving to the left, the right foot has to cross the left. And while the legs move sideways (to the left or to the right), the body still has to face into the circle, so we have to twist from the waist! We also practised dancing a chain, in a big circle - two bars for each hand. Then we practised the timing for a Grand Chain, in which we have six hands in eight bars: so we count Right, Left, Ri-i-ight, Left, Right, Le-e-eft.
  4. Pittenweem (R) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/5291/ 
  5. New Year Jig (J) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/17898/ Devised by Maureen Robson, for a childrens class. It includes the same Rsh reel followed by a chase as the previous dance. This is the first dance on the programme of the Easter Saturday dance. 
  6. Mr Graham W Donald (S) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/17463/ , devised for Graham Donald, who will be teaching the Teachers Workshop on Easter Weekend. A very nice, if somewhat tricky, Strathspey. It is on the programme of the Saturday night dance. 
  7. Follow me Home (J) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/2267/ Also a bit tricky, also on the Saturday night dance programme.
