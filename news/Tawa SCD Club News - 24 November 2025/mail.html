Tawa SCD Club News - 24 November 2025

Hello everyone

We had a lovely  'potluck' afternoon tea yesterday. Twenty of us gathered at Hilary's home in Aotea, and enjoyed admiring her new house and her garden. It was great to be able to chat with our dancing friends, as there isn't usually much time for this between dances on club nights. 

After a plentiful afternoon tea, the floor was cleared and Hilary and Jason prepared to play, so that we could do a few dances - Shifting' Bobbins (Anne's favourite), New Year Jig (a Tawa must-do), City of Belfast (everyone's favourite strathspey), The Minister on the Loch, Miss Johnstone of Ardrossan (one of my favourites), and The Water of Leith (also one of my faves). Catherine briefed them all, "off the top of her head", and  though not everyone danced, those who did, really enjoyed it.
