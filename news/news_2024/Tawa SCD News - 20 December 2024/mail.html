SUMMER SCHOOL UPDATES

Afternoon and Evening Activities

At Summer School, attendance at morning dance classes is the only thing that is compulsory. Registered commuters and residents can join in with the afternoon and evening activities as they want. There's a great range of optional activities during the afternoons to choose from—including general dance classes with international teachers, rock and roll, forums, excursions (extra charge), Thirtytwosome practice, 400 years of Scottish Dance Music in 60 minutes from musician Elke Baker...and more!

Download the Full School Programme to see everything that's on offer for commuters and residents.
 

Latest Newsletter

See the latest Summer School newsletter for more details of activities at the School including the New Zealand Branch events open to everyone—Teachers Forum, NZ Branch AGM, Meet the Management Committee and JAM Forum.
 

Registration for Commuters

On Saturday 28 December, Summer School registration at Scots College is from 1pm - 4pm. However, if it's more convenient, commuters can collect their name badges and welcome pack at the Opening Night dance.
 

Thirtytwosome Reel Practice

At Hogmanay, The Thirtytwosome Reel is danced immediately after the Hogmanay ceremony and will not be briefed. (Note: You will have the option of dancing the Eightsome if you wish).

If you are planning to go to Hogmanay (casually or as a school attendee) and want to do this dance, please go to the Thirtytwosome practice at 4pm on Tuesday 31 December in Scots College Hall. As an added bonus, there will also be a walkthrough of the Maxina.
 

Casual Attendees at Evening Functions: Payment

Unless you purchase a multi-pass entry to all evening events, you will need to pay in cash at the door for the evening functions.
 

McPhee's Celtic

Do you need new dancing shoes or other dancing attire? McPhee's Celtic will have a shop at Scots College in the McKinnon Room 1 on 29 and 30 December from 1pm-5.30pm (or later if there is demand). Great to stock up on dancing shoes, kilt hose, flashes and other dancing essentials. Dancers who aren't attending Summer School are welcome!

Find out all about Summer School on this website


RSCDS Dance Scottish Together
The monthly blog from the UK HQ can be found here. The "Dance Around the World" video includes snippets of NZ clubs dancing on 23 November, but our offering of "Oriel Strathspey", done at our potluck dinner, was not included, despite my sending it to them. 


Finally for 2024 ...
Wishing everyone a lovely Christmas, with family and friends, and if I don't see you at Hogmanay, best wishes for a happy, prosperous and dance-filled 2025!
