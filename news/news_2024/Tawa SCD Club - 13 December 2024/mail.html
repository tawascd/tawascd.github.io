The latest Summer School newsletter was sent out to attendees a few days ago, but I thought those who are not registered might want to have the details too, so it is attached below. 

I have had a further plea from Summer School organisers, asking for people to help with the teachers' exam candidates sessions. Jeanette Watson thanks those people who have already offered to assist the exam candidates with their practice and the exams, but she continues:

We do still have some spaces available - particularly for the morning exams, but all sessions could do with a few more bodies - so please have a chat to your friends and ask them to register too. At the moment, we are counting those who've signed up for particular sessions. If you've also noted that you might be available for other sessions, we're not counting on you yet, but we might need to, closer to the time. We'll let you know!

Please note that because of the number of candidates, the exam sessions are now 9am to 12.05pm and 1pm to 4.15pm.

 

You don't have to be a Summer School registered attendee. Look upon it as a free lesson! So a plea to our intermediate and advanced level dancers: please consider helping. It's for a good cause - SCD always need more teachers if we want to continue to have tutors for our clubs and for SCD to continue to be the great pastime that it is for so many people.
