Tawa SCD News - Summer School

Hello everyone

I have had a couple of communications from Summer School, via the Region secretary, which I should pass on::

The Summer School committee will be sending out their final pre-school newsletter soon, but they want to let people know that the closing date for casual meals at SS will be 13 December. This is of interests to people who are attending SS as commuters, or those who will only be attending evening events.

Casual Meals Commuters and casual attendees can sign up to share a meal at Scots College. This is a great way to socialise and can make life easier when you are busy with all the exciting things on offer. Prices are $15 breakfast, $22 lunch and $35 dinner. Details for booking are here: Summer School casual meals 

The SS committee is still looking for a gofer for the Social Class. Details of what gofers are expected to do, are in the document below. 

They are also looking for more people to be “stooges” at the teacher exams, particularly for the morning exams on 3 Jan, 9-12.30, but all slots could do with a few more. This involves being volunteer dancers in a class to be taught by teacher certificate candidates. Volunteers must be advanced or intermediate level dancers.  Details, and to register here. Tawa member Maureen Sullivan is one of the candidates, so let’s support her! 

The full programme for  Sumer School, plus various other important bits of information are in the second newsletter they sent out. It might be worth another look. Of course, there is more on the website: https://nzsummerschool2024.nz/ 

Cheers
