Tawa SCD Club News - 26 June 2025

Hello everyone

Another pretty wet night, but attendance was better than last week, with just over two five-couple sets. We did some interesting dances, some with a few challenges. 

Joy mentioned that the "Bowling Lake" was there again. A few weeks ago, she sent me a photo of the water on the greens, with the Tawa Pool all lit up in the background. She said "Snapped this as I left, so still". I think it's a great shot.
 



Tonight's dances 
  1. Warm-ups
  2. Ecclefechan Feline (J) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/12848/
  3. Gaelforce Wind (R) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/695/ This is the dance that has a Men's Chain.
  4. Memories (S) -  A 3C strathspey devised by Roy Goldring. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/12689/  It has some slightly unusual setting to corners, and hands across on the sides (like "teapots" but on the sides), and it finishes with an all round poussette. 
  5. The Morrison Measure (R) - A 5C reel by Roy Goldring. Elizabeth put this on (quick change of plan!) because our numbers were right for two five-couple sets. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/12690/
  6. Flora's Fancy (J) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/16533/
  7. The Swan and the Tay (S) - The name refers to two rivers that both flow through a city called Perth: The Swan in Perth Australia, and the Tay in Perth Scotland. Devised by a dancer in Perth Australia.  https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/14741/
  8. Scott Meikle (R) - With just five minutes left, we had time for one quick favourite. It was a bit hurried, and it got a bit muddled in one of the sets. We may do it again - properly - Elizabeth said ...

Reels Class
Catherine will be teaching a Reels Class, on Saturday 2 August, from 1:30 to 4:30 pm, at the Petone Bowling Club Roxburgh Street, Petone. 
This class will be open to members of all clubs. It will be for any dancers who would like to revisit the subtleties of all manner of reels - 3-couple, 4-couple reels; Right shoulder, Left Shoulder; reels on the side, reels across, reels on the diagonal; cross-over reels! If anyone still has doubts about reels, this is the class for you!
