Tawa SCD Club News - 1 August 2024

Hello everyone

Wow, what a great night. Despite it being so-o-o cold and wet, we had a good turn-out, with 18 members, eight beginners, and two visitors. Linda and Ivan Roxburgh from Mosgiel were visiting again, as the expected grandchild in Waiouru had not yet arrived. And we were delighted that all five of the beginners who had come last week and the week before, have come back, plus another three new ones! 

Catherine thanked the members for supporting the new people. It makes for good learning and enjoyable social interaction when each new person has a more experienced dancer to guide them through all the new stuff! This week, the beginners will be getting a separate email; next week it will all be together. 

And thank you to Karen who volunteered to help Jo with organising supper. Jo did come along despite not being able to dance because of a sprained ankle. Thanks Jo!
