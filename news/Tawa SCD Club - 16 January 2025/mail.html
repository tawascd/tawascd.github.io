Tawa SCD Club - 16 January 2025

Hello everyone

Happy New Year! I hope you have all had an enjoyable and relaxing holiday break  (despite the lack of summer in Wellington!). 

It is Thursday, so it must be time for a Tawa SCD Club update. 

The members who attended Summer School, either as residents, commuters or evenings-only attendees have hopefully recovered from the excitement and exhaustion by now. 
Here are a few photos and bits of news to share: 

News: Congratulations to Maureen S, who passed her Unit 3 exam. Well done!  And Tawa has another member on the Branch Executive - Debbie Stephens is the new Branch Treasurer - this is in addition to Fiona McC who is the Branch IT coordinator (since last year). And though not on the Exec, yours truly is the Branch Editor - now very busy preparing the NZ Scottish Country Dancer magazine. 

Now for some photos - all taken by Andrew Oliver, and available to see on Flickr:  Note also at the top of that page, links to other websites with Summer School photos.

Robert and Helen M were part of the team looking after the drinks and nibbles at President's reception.

Time to start thinking of the year ahead ... 

Summer Dancing 
Summer Dancing is starting next Tuesday, 21 January, at 7:00 pm. 
This is dancing on the lawn in front of the Old Government Building (the VUW Law School), on Lambton Quay. It is very casual, and a great way to get feet and brains back into dancing mode. Footwear: you can wear your dancing shoes, sneakers, or no shoes at all! 
It goes for four Tuesdays, 21 and 28 January, and 4 and 11 February, starting at 7:00 pm, till 8:45 pm. Cost: Gold coin donation. If the weather is unsuitable, it will be cancelled, as there is no alternative inside venue. Check the Wellington Region website for cancellations.



Tawa pre-season potluck dinner
Before we start dancing again on the first Thursday of March (6 March), we will have a potluck dinner at Caroline Sherborne's home on Saturday 22 February. 
Details to come closer to the time, but please mark your diaries! 

Tawa Club Nights 
Start again on Thursday 6 March, 7:30 pm, at the usual venue, Tawa Bowling Club, 13 Davies St, Tawa.

I hope everyone will be keen to come back, including our 2024 'new dancers'.And any dancers who, for whatever reason, haven't been attending for a while, will of course be welcomed back with open arms! 

Tawa Club AGM
The club's AGM will be held on a club night, on Thursday 3 April, 2025. 

That's all for now folks!
