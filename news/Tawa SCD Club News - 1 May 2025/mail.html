Feedback requested by the Wellington Region committee
From Brenda Vale, Wellington Region Secretary 
1. On Region classes and events:
The region is seeking feedback on whether the current region classes and events are fulfilling the needs of Wellington club members.
If you could send feedback on this together with ideas for change by Friday 30th May that would be much appreciated as these can then be considered at the region June meeting. 

Note: this refers to the Region classes - Basics, Intermediate and Advanced - and also to the special sessions, such as the recent Early Dances Day, the forthcoming "Fugues Day" (11 October), and the New Dancers' Celebration preparation classes. 

2. On the future of NZ Summer Schools:
At the Branch AGM, a document was produced (attached) about the future of NZ Summer Schools. One suggestion is that Summer School would alternate between Christchurch and Wellington as this would organisationally probably be more efficient. However, it could be more onerous for clubs in terms of providing helpers and baking. The region is seeking feedback from clubs as to how members feel about such a suggestion. This feedback will then be collated and passed back to the Branch. 
The link to the Branch information is below but I have also downloaded and attached the relevant document. The missing figures for Wellington are 151 registrations of which 81 were resident.
https://dancescottish.org.nz/branch-agm-reports/

If you have any feedback on either issue, please send them to me (Desiree), and I will pass on your comments.
