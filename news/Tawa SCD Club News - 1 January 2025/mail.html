Tawa SCD Club News - 1 January 2025

Hello everyone

Happy New Year to all of you. I hope 2025 will bring you everything you hope and wish for, including lots of dancing!

Just keeping you up to date - Summer School has reached the half-way point, with a day free of dancing, for people to nurse their sore feet and maybe catching up on some sleep!.

I'm not attending the school, but have been enjoying the evening events. I know Joy, Colin and Helen M are attending the full school as residents. And Robert and Brenda, and I think Fiona L, are attending the second half of the school was commuters. Maureen has been attending the teachers' exam class as a commuter and I have not seen her at the evening dances - as the candidates are working very hard and are given "homework", so no time to actually dance! 

I was pleased to see Maureen Robson at the "Youth Night". She actually danced three strathspeys, and was feeling very happy. But I was disappointed to see very few Tawa members at the four events I have attended so far. Perhaps you are all saving it up for tomorrow night - The President's Ball. 

The President's Ball - 2 January 2025  (https://nzsummerschool2024.nz/evening-functions/#PresidentsBall)
In the Scots College Gym (there will be a 'balloon trail' to show the way from the carpark to the gym)
Starts: at 7:00pm, with Drinks and nibbles
MC: Catherine McCutcheon
Music: Elke Baker, Lisa Scott and Richard Hardie (They were terrific on opening night!) 
Then a "Grand March", followed by dancing until 11pm.
Cost: RSCDS $30; Non RSCDS $35; Spectators $20 

This is the night that Tawa is helping out. Catherine will be the MC, and Robert, Brenda, Helen M and Fiona L will be helping to serve the drinks and nibbles before the dance, and the supper at half-time. I will be on door duty. I am hoping for a great turn-out of Tawa members. A great opportunity to experience a "grand march" to start proceedings, with piper Nicole Trewavas leading the march. 

I hope that many of you who are in Wellington right now will be able to come along and enjoy a very special event. To encourage you, I am attaching a cribsheet, with links to videos. 

See you there!
