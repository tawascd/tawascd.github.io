Tawa SCD Club News - 27 November 2025

Hello everyone

It's Thursday, so it must be time for a Tawa SCD Club newsletter, even though we have finished our club nights. But this is probably the last newsletter you will get from me this year.

There are just a couple more Upcoming Events to remind you of, for this year.

This Saturday, 29 November, 7:30 pm - Carterton SCD Club's 25th Anniversary Ball, Wairarapa Events Centre, Holloway Street, Carterton. The poster and crib sheet are attached below. 
Sunday 30 November, 11:00 am - Carterton's St Andrew's Day Brunch. For those that are staying in the Wairarapa overnight. However, the date to put your name down for this was on 22 November. If you haven't RSVP'ed, but are desperate to go anyway, you can try emailing Elaine Laidlaw: elaine@laidlaw.net.nz

And then there will be HOGMANAY, the New Year's Eve celebration with a Scottish flavour. Great fun, don't miss it, if you are gong to be in Wellington on NY's Eve. Non-dancing partners may enjoy it too. 

Wednesday 31 December, 8:00 pm - 1:30 am - Hogmanay 2025, Crofton Downs School, Cardwell. The programme and cribsheet are attached below. 


Summer Dancing 
I don't know if there will be Summer Dancing in the new year. It usually happens over four Tuesdays in January/February on the lawn in front of the Old Government Buildings, on Lambton Quay, but I haven't heard if this will be happening. If anything eventuates, I will be sure to let you know. 


Photos and videos of Tawa's final Night - by Joy Tracey
Joy has posted photos and videos  of our Final Night on Facebook. The link is here.   Her FB page is called "JET Scottish Country Dance Photos". 
The videos are of Anna Holden's Strathspey, New Year Jig, Domino Five, and Seann Truibhas Willichan. 
And if you scroll down a bit further, you will find photos and videos of the Ngaio Club's final night. 


Are you still looking for that special Christmas gift for someone - or yourself?
I hope you don't mind my doing a bit of advertising for my potter sister, Aimee McLeod. She is having an Open Studio this weekend - 29 and 30 November, 10 am to 4 pm.,  at 35 Huntleigh Park Way, Ngaio. You'll recognise her place by the mosaics on her garden steps. She has a huge range of items and styles - from domestic ware (mugs, plates bowls, vases) to decorative garden pots and statues. Do go and check her out. Also her FB page.


She will also have a stall at the Thorndon Fair, on Sunday 5 December, 10 am  - 3 pm, Tinakori Road. Her stall is usually right outside the Shepherd's Arms Hotel. 

 


Finally ...
And now, really finally, all that remains, is to wish you all a very enjoyable festive season, a pleasant Christmas, spent in the way that gives you the most pleasure, and best wishes from John and me for a great 2026. I hope to see many of you at Hogmanay! 

I look forward to everyone coming back to Tawa SCD Club in the new year.

Cheers
Desiree
