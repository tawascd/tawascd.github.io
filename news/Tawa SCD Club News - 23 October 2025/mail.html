Tawa SCD Club News - 23 October 2025

Hello everyone

I hope you are all well, warm and safe tonight. It was quite a day wasn't it, with the extreme winds, and the "red" (i.e. potentially dangerous) wind warning. 

We're sorry that we felt we had to cancel tonight's class. Perhaps we pulled the plug a little too early, as would you believe it, it is relatively calm out there right now. However, I suspect people will have been be quite happy to remain cosily ensconced in their homes tonight. 

I hope Catherine made it safely to Christchurch, where she will be leading a Labour Weekend workshop for prospective SCD teachers. 

Despite there being no class tonight, you are getting a newsletter anyway. I had already written most of it, so you may as well have it.


Jan Bruce
On Tuesday, I had a call from Maureen Robson, our former teacher, to let me know that Jan Bruce had a heart attack last Friday. She had a stent put in and was due to have a further procedure yesterday. Maureen said that when she saw Jan, she seemed to be doing OK, and looked in reasonably good spirits. 
For those newer members, who may not know Jan - she is one of our Life Members, and was the club's President for many years before she stopped dancing. 
I am sure we will all join me in wishing her a speedy recovery. I have sent her a get well card on behalf of our club.
