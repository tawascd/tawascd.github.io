Tawa SCD Club News - 9 February 2025

Hello everyone

As we are coming up to the beginning of the dancing years, here is what is happening:

Potluck dinner - Saturday 22 February
It is less than two weeks to go till our pre-season, welcome-back potluck dinner on 22 February. I do hope you have marked your diaries for this event. Here are the details:

  • Our hosts will again be Caroline and John Sherborne, who made us very welcome for a successful potluck this time last year. 
  • The address is: redacted, Tawa. Their house is at the end of a long drive, and there is parking at the top, but not a whole lot, so if people can share rides, so much the better. 
  • Start arriving from 5pm, on Saturday 22 February. We aim to eat around 6pm. 
  • Bring your favourite dish to share, and a drink if you're that way inclined.
  • Everyone is welcome, and that of course includes our new-ish dancers, who started in August last year. 

Caroline would like an indication of the numbers that are likely to come along (to make sure she has enough plates, cutlery, etc). So please let me know, by Thursday 20 Feb if you are planning to come along. 


Summer Dancing
There is only one more Summer Dancing night - on Tuesday 11 February, on the lawn in front of the Old Government Building (VUW Law School) on Lambton Quay. The last Summer Dancing three nights have been perfect weather-wise, and were most enjoyable. 


Start of club nights
Our dancing club nights resume on Thursday 6 March,  at 7:30 pm, at the Tawa Bowling Club.
Please come a little early to catch up with friends, and to get your dancing shoes on, and be ready for Catherine to start the class at 7:30.
We look forward to seeing you all back!

To those people that we haven't seen at club for some time during the past year, please be assured that you will be very welcome to come come back. If, however, you will not be returning, and you no longer wish to receive the Tawa Club newsletters, please let me know. 

Looking forward to seeing lots of you at the potluck.
