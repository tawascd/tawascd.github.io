Tawa SCD Club News - 26 December 2025

Hello everyone

I hope you have all had a lovely Chrismas, and are enjoying Boxing Day today (despite the less than ideal weather).

For those that are going to be around Wellington on New Year's Eve, here is a reminder of the Hogmanay Dance.

It will be held on 31 December (obviously!), starting at 8 pm, and continuing till 1:30 am. The venue will be the Crofton Downs School, Chartwell Drive (off Churchill Drive), Crofton Downs. The poster and crib sheet are attached below. 

Entry is a small plate and $18 RSCDS, $20 Non-RSCDS, $5 JAMs and Spectators. It will be totally suitable to bring along your non-dancing partner for a great night.

For those of you heading to Summer School - have a great time! And anyone travelling - be safe on the road and in the sun (if you get any!).

Wishing you all an excellent 2026, filled with dance, friendship and fun. And we'll hopefully see you at club in the new year.   

Cheers
Desiree
