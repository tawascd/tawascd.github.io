Tawa SCD Club News - 13 November 2025

Hello everyone

What a great night we had at Ngaio's Final Night. There were five sets of dancers, with Tawa members accounting for two sets. The stage was decorated with tartan rugs, tartan banners and tartan bunting on some of the walls. Jason and Hilary made great music, including Jason playing the tuba for the, by now 'traditional' for Ngaio, Trip to Bavaria, in which four men doff their 'Bavarian' hats as they pass each other.  

The evening was progressing so well, that there was time for an extra dance that was not on the programme: three sets took to the floor for Maxwell's Rant, before we danced the final dance, De'il Amang the Tailors. As usual, Melva made the evening fun with spot prizes and balloons that had to be kept aloft by the dancing couples in the Barmkin.  
The evening finished with a plentiful supper. A big thank you to Melva and the members of Ngaio Club for a lovely evening's dancing. 

John took photos, and I think Joy took some videos, which we will hopefully get to see in due course.
