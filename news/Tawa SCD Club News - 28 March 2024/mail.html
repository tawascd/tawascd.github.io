Tawa SCD Club News - 28 March 2024

Hello everyone

We had a lovely evenings dancing tonight, with live music from Lynne, Mary and Cassandra, who will also be playing at the Saturdays Easter dance. Sharlene was unable to play because of a family emergency, so Cassandra had to step in at the last minute. With very little time to practise, didnt she do well, especially in Toast to the Mousies !

We had quite good numbers to begin with (27 dancers), but many left after the Notices, so we were left with just two sets.
