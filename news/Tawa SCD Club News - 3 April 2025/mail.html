Tonight's dances
  1. All Things Nice (R ) - Our walking warm-up dance caused a fair bit of confusion and hilarity in the promenade to find another couple to dance with. A great mixer! https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/21577/ It includes a Ladies' Chain. Catherine put this on the programme specially for our one remaining new dancer, but she wasn't there tonight! But the best of us enjoyed the general chaos!
  2. Beach Dancer (J) - This is on our Annual Dance programme. It also includes a Ladies' Chain. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/11587/ 
  3. City of Belfast (S) - A great favourite, which includes an Espagnole. A bit of extra time spent on perfecting the Espagnole. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/13847/ I especially like the 3rd video, which shows the 'shape' of the formations - especially the fact that the reel across is done in 2nd place, finishing with the 2s dancing into 1st place, and the 2s being able to dance from 2nd place to face their 1st corners, without any awkward wriggling around each other (which happens when the reel is done in 1st place). 
  4. The Laird of Milton's Daughter (J) - This is on our Annual Dance programme. It incudes a corner chain. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/3659/ 
  5. Campbell of Cawdor (S) - "A nice teaching dance" said Catherine, as it includes both a Ladies' Chain, and a Half Diamond Poussette. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/974/ Before dancing this we practised doing double strathspey setting steps: i.e. doing two setting steps to the right, then two to the left, requiring the 'leading' foot to be 'pushed out of the way' by the other foot before the second setting step. This was an introduction to a "foot change" required by one in each couple when dancing a half diamond poussette, which we practised next. While a complete (all round) diamond poussette is not a progression, a half poussette is a progression - i.e. the couples finish in reversed places. 
  6. The Luckenbooth Brooch (J) - The name refers to a traditional piece of Scottish jewellery that symbolises love and devotion. Go to the 'Extra info' on the link to see a picture of one. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/3980/ 
  7. Starlight (R) - We haven't done this dance before, and I really liked it! https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/11769/ It includes double triangles with a 'meanwhile' and finishes with hello-goodbye setting.
