Tawa SCD Club News - 25 September 2025

Hello everyone

Tonight's dances
The first five dances were for everyone, and No 6-8 were for the more seasoned dancers. Before dancing "Reel Easy" (No 5), we had some step practice to learn Pas de Basque, the fifth of the five SCD steps. 
  1. Come What May (J) - This was our walking warm-up dance. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/17900/  
  2. Across Kinness Burn (R) - Devised by Iain Boyd. The description is not available online, but as I remember it started with a fig of 8 on the side, followed by mirror reels. 
  3. Mrs Stewart Sinton's Reel (J) - Called a reel, but it is actually a jig. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/4804/
  4. Autumn Leaves (S) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/316/ 
  5. Reel Easy (R) - The description states that there is a R Sh reel in this, but Catherine modified it to a mirror reel, to make it easier for Ken. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/13827/ 
  6. The Diamond Jubilee (J) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/1647/ 
  7. Wisp of Thistle (S) - This is on the programme for the New Dancers' Celebration.https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/7204/ 
  8. The Zoologist (J) - Quite a busy, fast-moving dance. It finishes with a snake pass. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/13590/
