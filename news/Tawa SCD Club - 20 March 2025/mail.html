Tonight's dances
  1. Happy to Meet (J) - Devised by Iain Boyd, https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/2782/ 
  2. The Barmkin (R) - An 88-bar, 4C Reel in a square set. Devised by Roy Goldring. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/413/ 
  3. Neidpath Castle (S) - This is on Tawa's Annual Dance programme. This is the dance that Catherine's calls a "meatball sandwich".  https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/4890/ 
  4. The March Hare (R) - There are several dances called The March hare. I suspect that you danced Iain Boyd's version.  https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/4142/ 
  5. The Wild Geese (J) - This will have been the nod to St Patrick's Day (last Monday, 17 March), as the title refers to Irish soldiers who have fought in the service of various foreign kings. (Read the Extra info in the link). https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/7147/ 
  6. Seann Truibhas Willichan (S) - The title means "William's old trousers". https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/5982/ 
  7. Fire Hose Reel (R) - This is on Tawa's Annual Dance programme. I am sorry I missed out on dancing it. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/18209/ 
  8. The Waipu Wanderers (R) - Devised by NZer, the late Eddy West. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/15683/
