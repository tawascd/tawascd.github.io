Tawa SCD Club News - 12 March 2026

Hello everyone

Wasn't today a lovely day? And yesterday too. Let's hope that we will get a few more nice days like this before autumn sets in properly. 

We had just one 4C and one 5C set tonight. A few people have dropped out, and some are still away. Hopefully we will get a full muster soon.


Tonight's dances
  1. Happy to Meet (J) - This was our walking warm-up dance. Devised by Wellington teacher Iain Boyd https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/2782/
  2. Lady Catherine Bruce's Reel (J) - Though it's called a Reel, its' actually a Jig. Before dancing this Catherine had us practise a 2C Allemande. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/3594/
  3. The Paisley Weavers (S) - This dance starts with two chords, the first for the bow/curtsey, the second for the 2C to cross to the opposite side. The dance includes a Grand Chain, followed by a Ladies' Chain. Before dancing this we practised the Ladies' Chain. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/13727/ Danced last week. 
  4. Mrs Stewart's Jig (J) - Also danced last week. Has the same combination of Grand Chain followed by a Ladies' Chain, but this is in fast time. 
  5. Allemande to Go (R) - Devised by Gaye Collin. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/16844/
  6. Friendships (S) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/15411/
  7. The Society Piper (R) - https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/6177/
  8. Napier's Index (J) - Keith Napier created a computerised index of SC Dances. https://my.strathspey.org/dd/dance/10535/
