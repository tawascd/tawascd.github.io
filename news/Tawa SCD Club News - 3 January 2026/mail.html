And now, a reminder of upcoming dancing opportunities in Wellington - Summer Dancing will be happening again on the lawn in front of the Old Government Building on Lambton Quay, at 7:00pm. The dates are the last two Tuesdays of January (20, 27 Jan) and the first two Tuesdays in February (3, 10 Feb).    

These are always very casual - you can wear your dancing shoes, or sneakers or bare feet - and a great opportunity to catch up with other dancers after the holiday break. The poster is here.
